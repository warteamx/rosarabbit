## üñãÔ∏è Overview

*Radical Acceptance* is a blend of Buddhist wisdom, Western psychology, and heart-centered mindfulness practice. In this transformative book, clinical psychologist and meditation teacher Tara Brach introduces the concept of ‚ÄúRadical Acceptance‚Äù ‚Äî the practice of embracing ourselves, just as we are, with compassion and awareness.

She argues that many people live in a ‚Äútrance of unworthiness,‚Äù believing they are fundamentally flawed or not good enough. By learning to meet every moment ‚Äî and every part of ourselves ‚Äî with openness and compassion, we awaken from this trance and begin to live with genuine freedom and presence.

Brach‚Äôs work is grounded in mindfulness-based cognitive therapy, self-compassion research, and trauma healing.
